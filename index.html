How the game works

  • Each level teaches one beginner cybersecurity concept.
  • Students submit the exact flag to earn points and pass the level.
  • Hints are allowed because learning is the goal, not gatekeeping.
  • Progress is saved in the browser with local storage.
Built for cybersecurity students who are learning CTF fundamentals one flag at a time.
